Why Us

Doing a common thing uncommonly well, brings success.!

Customer Focus

We rethink every business process, product and service from the standpoint of our customer, so as to exceed expectations at every touch point. We have built long term relations and work hand in hand with our valued customers to ensure that they get the best value for their money.

We believe and follow

TQM (Total Quality Management) and Kaizen(Continuous Improvement) to attain Total Customer Satisfaction.No compromise in quality and Total customer satisfaction are the pillars of our success and aptly demonstrated by our reputed clientele which spans from governments to NGOs and spreads across various private institutions.

Company Profile

Taking business to the global level.!

Amazon Exports Hub is a global export and import firm spread across continents. It is efficiently managed by a core team of dynamic professionals who are Subject Matter Experts. We are the creators of 12-SIGMA Methodology for Exports which is being refined iteratively.

We believe: Success in business requires discipline, honesty and hard work to exceed our customers expectations. With this motto, we have established fruitful and long term relationships with our customers.

We are a highly respected group of individuals who are well versed in foreign trade processes and international trade.

History

History. In the past and the present.!

Amazon Exports Hub was established in 2004, in Africa. Over the years, we have become a conglomerate, an umbrella of companies with diversified businesses due to the trust, integrity and reliable reputation we have built with our customers. Today, we have operations in USA, Canada, Africa, India.

We started very small but have expanded across continents today. From a small one room office, we have achieved new heights due to our dedication and determination. Learning from our promoters, who have always said: You don’t have to be Great to start but you have to Start to be GREAT.
